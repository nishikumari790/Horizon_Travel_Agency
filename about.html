Our Story

Born from a Passion for Travel

Founded in 2008 by seasoned traveler and explorer, Manisha Dipti Sharma HorizonTravel began as a small boutique agency with one simple mission: to make luxury travel accessible, personal, and truly transformative.

What started as a team of four passionate travel enthusiasts has grown into a global network of 100+ destination specialists, serving over 10,000 satisfied travelers across 95 countries. Every journey we craft is infused with the same love for discovery that inspired our founding.
